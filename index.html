Let's go back through those images again...

Chubby Checker?

"Come on baby, let's do The Twist..."

Chubby Checker, as was the case with many African American musical artists in the late 1950s and early 1960s, had to take care to cultivate a "safe" image in appearance and dress.

What we can take from this is that it is vital to recognize that interpersonal communication occurs on multiple layers (how a performing artist looks affects the way the music is perceived), and that any message carries with it potential bias, signification, and missed meaning, whether due to errors in one or both of transmission and reception.

This can happen with any interaction among members of a team.
